Welcome Page

This is the welcome page that you will see when you first approach the tablet at the gym entrance.

Tap anywhere on the tablet screen to be brought to the main dashboard page of the app.

Dashboard

When you tap anywhere on the welcome page, you will be directed to this page.

Here, you have the options to:

Sign in/out (existing user or admin)

Create a user account.

Go back to the cover (welcome) page.

Create an Account

To create an account, click on “Don’t have an account?”


After Selecting “Don’t have an account?”, you will be brought to this page to create one:

To create an account, you will need to fill out these required fields:

    First Name
    Last Name
    Organization
    Email
    Username
    Password
    Agree to liability waiver

You can select Liability Waiver to view the waiver.

After selecting “Create Account”, your account will automatically be signed in, and your profile will read “checked out” on the left side of the dashboard. You will also receive a confirmation email.

Sign In or Out

To sign in to the application, click the green “Sign In” button.


After selecting “Sign In”, you will be brought to a page to enter your credentials.



After entering your credentials, click “Log In”.





Check In our Out

If you are checked out, you will see a prompt that allows you the check in. If you are currently checked in, you will see a prompt that allows you to check out.


**FOR USERS**

Press the button accordingly.



**FOR ADMINS**

You will be taken to a page to view the Admin dashboard. Click the button to enter the admin dashboard.



Logged In

When logged in, you will be asked how long you plan on using the gym, fill this section out accordingly.

User List

The User List displays all currently created accounts, their status (Checked in or Checked Out) and estimated end times for active users.

After entering your estimated time in the gym, you will see your username “Checked In” on the right side of the dashboard with the estimated time you will be done using the gym.

After logging your exercise routine and logging out, you will see your username "Checked Out" on the right side of the dashboard.

Log Exercises or Just Log Me Out

Here, you will be able to choose if you would like to log what you did in the gym, or you can skip straight to “just log me out”.

If you choose to not record your workout, you have the option to jump straight to “Just Log Me Out”.



Log Exercises

If you select “Log Daily Exercises”, this is the page you will see.

From here, you can:

Choose the equipment that you used

Duration you used it

Weight you used

Sets

Reps

You can add multiple exercises by clicking the "Add" button

When you have logged your routine, clicking the "Log/Email Exercises and Sign Out" button will save your exercise routine, send you an email of your daily routine and check you out.

Admin Login

Signing in as an admin is the same procedure as signing in as a user

Manage Users

The Manage Users tab allows admins to enter a username, select a date range and view the exercise logs for that user during the given date range.

Manage Admins

The Manage Admins tab allows admins to create a new admin account. The process is the same as creating a new user, except this account will have administrative privileges.

Manage Equipment

The Manage Equipment tab allows admins to add new equipment to the app by typing in the name of the equipment and clicking the "Add" button. The list of created equipment will be listed and the red trashcan icons to the right allow you to delete the equipment. When new equipment is added or deleted, the list of available equipment in the dropdown menu of the "Exercise Logging" modal will reflect the list on this page.

User Logs

When an admin looks up a user, the "Workout Data Logs" modal will pop up with a list of drop down menus of the logged exercise routines for the user and the given date range. The admin also has the authority to delete a user's account on this page.
